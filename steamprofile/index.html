My history with Valve titles

I have been a hardcore gamer for as far as I can tell. I remember my dad giving me a copy of Half Life Game of the Year edition when I was 9, which included a shirt that I couldn't wear until I was around 15 because it didn't fit. I still have the CD.

When I was 12, I poked a hole in between my bedroom and my brother's to pass a LAN cable through the wall and play Team Fortress Classic with him. Then came Counter Strike, then TF2 and then Dota 2 which I play almost every day. In fact, I have to thank Steam because I am still able to play with the same group of friends with whom I played CS 1.5 with ages ago, even though some of them live halfway across the globe.
